Fried Udon

  • Udon noodles
  • Thin pork cutlets
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spring onions
  • Fish tofu
  • Fish cake
  • Eggs
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Mirin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Slice fish tofu and fish cake, then set aside
  2. Make a scrambled egg, break apart and set aside
  3. Boil udon and set aside
  4. Dice onions, slice spring onions, and dice garlic
  5. Add oil to a pan at medium heat
  6. Add onions and garlic
  7. Add pork
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Add the fish tofu and fish cake
  10. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and mirin
  11. Finally, add the udon and eggs
