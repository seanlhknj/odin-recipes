Udon Soup

  • Udon
  • Sliced pork or beef
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Fish balls
  • Fish tofu
  • Fish cake
  • Spring Onion
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Dashi
  • Salt
  1. Boil eggs for roughly 7 minutes
  2. Put eggs in cold water and peel them
  3. If you have time, marinate them in soy sauce and mirin
  4. Pour water into a pot and boil
  5. Add dashi, salt, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and mirin
  6. Add your toppings to the stock
  7. Add the udon noodles
  8. Add spring onions and eggs
