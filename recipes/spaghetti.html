Spaghetti

  • Spaghetti
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Paprika
  • Parmesan cheese
  1. Pour water in a pot, add salt, and boil
  2. Add spaghetti and boil until al dente
  3. Drain the spaghetti, save some pasta water and set aside
  4. Dice onions and garlic
  5. Put oil in a pan at medium heat
  6. Add onions and garlic
  7. Add ground beef and break it apart
  8. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, and a little bit of sugar
  9. Once the meat is browned, add the pasta sauce and let it simmer
  10. Combine the sauce with the spaghetti and add cheese
